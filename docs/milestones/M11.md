## Acceptance criteria

- Agents can investigate without mutation.
- Safety boundaries remain consistent with `AGENTS.md` and active docs.
- Scope is limited to this milestone and its active plan.
- Validation evidence is recorded in the active plan and status docs.
- Handoff notes identify what changed, what did not change, and the next safe thread.

## Validation expectations

Run evidence-grounding, hallucination, read-only query, token tracking, and agent-run tests.

Future agent components should log enough metadata for MoneyFlowBench ablation reporting, including model choice, whether critic review was enabled, whether evidence IDs were required, and whether graph or replay evidence was available.
