## When to use

Use when designing replay sessions, state reconstruction, simulations, or repair validation.

## Required checks

- Confirm replay inputs are explicit and evidence-linked.
- Confirm replay is deterministic and repeatable.
- Confirm repair claims require replay validation.
- Record artifacts needed for reproduction.

## CausalLedger invariants

- Replay contributes to financial truth.
- Agents cannot replace replay with narrative explanation.

## Output behavior

Report reproducibility risks, missing inputs, and validation commands needed.
